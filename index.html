About Me

I am a certified web developer with a diverse background that bridges the worlds of hospitality and technology. For over six years, I worked in the hospitality industry, where I honed valuable customer service skills and developed a strong work ethic. During this time, I discovered a passion for web development and programming, sparking my desire to pursue a career in tech. Prior to my time in hospitality, I earned a diploma in sports fitness from Northlink College, which helped shape my discipline and focus. Driven by my growing interest in coding, I made the decision to save up and enroll in a bootcamp with Hyperion Development to formally develop my programming skills. Today, I am working as a web developer at Trade245, a Forex brokerage, where I continue to build on my technical expertise and passion for web development.

Crypto Background

As a web developer, I have an affinity for the technical aspects of the crypto world, and I’m particularly drawn to discovering and understanding emerging technologies within the space.
